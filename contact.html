Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Typically, logo designs take 1-2 weeks, websites 3-6 weeks, and video projects 2-4 weeks. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing varies based on project requirements and scope. We offer tiered packages for most services, and we're happy to provide a custom quote after understanding your specific needs. Check our pricing page for more details.

Do you offer revisions on your work?

Yes, all our service packages include a specific number of revision rounds. Our standard packages typically include 2-3 rounds of revisions, while premium packages offer unlimited revisions within a reasonable timeframe.

What information do you need to start a project?

To get started, we'll need your project goals, target audience information, brand guidelines (if available), content requirements, and any specific preferences. Our detailed creative brief will guide you through providing all necessary information.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Yes, we work with clients worldwide. Our process is designed to accommodate remote collaboration with clear communication channels, regular updates, and flexible meeting schedules across different time zones.

What happens after my project is completed?

After project completion, we provide all necessary files and documentation. We offer 30 days of support for any minor adjustments. For websites, we offer optional maintenance packages. We're always available for future updates or new projects.
